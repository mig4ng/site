about

Hi, I’m Anirudh.

But you’ll see me as icyphox on the Internet. I’m doing my undergrad right now, majoring in CS. My primary interest is computer security, and more specifically — offensive security and digital forensics. On the less technical side of things, I love discussing operational security and tradecraft. I’m also a CTF player/security researcher at Sector443, an infosec community at my University. Sometimes, I will compose a post for my blog.

In my free time (which I tend to have a lot of) I contribute to open-source projects like the Nim programming language. Take a look at my GitHub profile for more of my work.
